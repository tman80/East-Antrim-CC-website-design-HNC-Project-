Club Rides

Weekend Club Rides

Our weekend club rides leave from Ballyearl leisure centre every Saturday and Sunday mornings at 10am.

Routes

Depending on wind direction our club rides are mainly based around the Antrim coast and Crumlin direction.

Distance covered

Each week the distance varies depending on route. The A group aim for 60/80 miles, B group 40/60 miles and C group 20/40 miles.
