Frequently Asked Questions

Click on questions below to reveal and hide answers

Question 1. How to navigate about the website
Answer. To navigate about the website just use the menu navigation bar which is located on each page. You can access any page from this navigation bar on the website. It doesn't matter which page you are on, each page is linked to all the other pages on the website.

Question 2. Where to find information about club rides
Answer. To find out information about club rides first click on the menu navigation bar. When the menu bar drops down, click on the club rides option and you will then directed into the club rides page. Here you will find general information regarding club rides, if you need more information regarding club rides etc just contact us using the form on the contact us page.

Question 3. How to find photo albums on our website
Answer. Firstly click on menu navigation bar and a drop down will appear. Then click on gallery to access the gallery page. Once inside the gallery page, click on photo or text and you will be redirected to the chosen album. You can also be redirected to see all our photo albums if you click on the See All link. This will link you to our Facebook social media page where all our albums are stored. To get back to our website just hit the back arrow located in the top left of the browser bar.

Question 4. How to contact someone in the cycling club
Answer. When on homepage, click the menu navigation bar and a drop down menu will appear. Then click on on the Contact US text and you will be taken to the contact us page. On the form on the left hand side enter your name, email address, subject and finally what your query is. Your details will then be sent to the dedicated email address and one of our members will get back to you in due course. All details entered on the form on the contact us page, will be held in the strictest of confidence in line with current laws and regulations. If you make a mistake on the contact us form just hit the reset button and all details on the form will be cleared for you to start again. If you would like to speak to someone directly, our club secretary's contact details are located in the footer section of each page.

Question 5. How to report an issue or problem with our website
Answer. If you come across any problems while using our site, please contact us using the form on our Contact Us page and tell us what the issue is. Firstly click on menu navigation bar then when the drop down menu appears click on the contact us option. Then use the form provided. We appreciate you taking the time to report faults or issues regarding the site as it will help us improve the website going forward.

Question 6. How to access social media accounts
Answer. Links to our social media accounts can be found in the bottom left hand corner or each page. To access each account just click on your preferred choice. This will redirect you to the chosen social media account on a new browser page.

Question 7. How to find where the club is located and where club rides begin
Answer. Firstly, click on the menu navigation bar, when the drop menu appears, click on contact us option and you will then see the interactive map on the right hand side of the page which shows the location of Ballyearl Leisure Centre. This is where we hold all club meetings and where are club rides begin and end.

Question 8. Where to find general cycling information
Answer. While on homepage, click on the menu button and a drop down menu will appear. From here, click on the safe cycling option and in here you will find a whole host of cycling information from general riding to road racing etc. Links to all the different pieces of information can be found at the top left hand corner of the screen. Once you have finished reading a particular section just click on the "return to top" link and it will bring you bring to the top of the page again.

Question 9. How to link to club sponsor website
Answer. On the homepage click on the audi logo image and you will be redirected to their website on a new page.

Question 10. How to buy club membership and a cycling license
Answer. If you decide to join the club, you can buy club membership and a cycling license from Cycling Ireland. Just click on the Cycling Ireland logo on the hompage and it will redirect you to the Cycling Ireland membership page. Once on the page just click the Join Now button and enter your details. If your having problems feel free to contact us and we will happily assist you with your registration.
