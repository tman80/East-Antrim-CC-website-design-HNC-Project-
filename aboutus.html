About Us

East Antrim Cycling Club is a recreational club that promotes cycling at all levels. We welcome both novice and seasoned riders alike. We aim to accommodate all levels and we will never leave you to ride home alone. Give us a go and start riding in a group of like minded people. Currently we have club rides on a Saturday and Sunday leaving from Ballyearl Leisure Centre at 10am. These rides are tailored to suit all levels and depending on numbers we will normally have three runs, an A Run, B Run and a C run.

The C Run is at a slower pace and will help to develop each individualâ€™s performance and stamina. We normally stop for coffee during the ride so everybody has a chance to refuel. We are a social club and our rides are tailored to be friendly, fun and at times hilly. Our aim is to teach people about cycling, improve fitness and meet new people. During the winter months we also hold club spin classes every Tuesday and Thursday nights from 7.30pm.

This a great way to keep up the fitness levels during the dark nights. After the winter months we plan to have two training rides during the week, most likely a Tuesday evening and Thursday evening. These rides will be posted up on our Facebook page to inform club members about what is happening during any particular week. Everybody is welcome so come along and see for yourself.
